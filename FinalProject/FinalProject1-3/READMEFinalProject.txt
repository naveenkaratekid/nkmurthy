README


For Mac Users, you must look at the lsqlite3Xcode image attached and follow instructions.

Make sure SQLite3 is installed on Mac.

On Mac Terminal, go to the folder where project is. Go to folder where source and header files are. On the terminal, enter “sqlite3 db.sqlite”. (NO QUOTES AROUND COMMAND”


To see tables in database:

enter “.tables” (NO QUOTES AROUND COMMAND)

To select everything from a particular table:
select * from tableName; (SEMICOLON IS IMPORTANT IN GENERAL COMMANDS (update)(select))
